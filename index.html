Programs We Offer

De La Salle Araneta University offers undergraduate programs within its four eminent colleges—College of Veterinary Medicine and Agricultural Sciences, College of Business, Management, and Accountancy, College of Education, and College of Arts, Sciences, and Technology. These undergraduate programs provide students with the requisite knowledge and training that make them invaluable assets for both local and international companies and organizations, as well as solid academic preparations that enable to pursue further studies.

College of Veterinary Medicine & Agricultural Sciences

The College of Veterinary Medicine and Agricultural Sciences (CVMAS) aims to provide quality education and excellence in veterinary medicine, agricultural sciences, and food technology with emphasis on companion animal practice, animal welfare, animal health, crop and livestock production, food processing and safety, environmental management, extension service, and research to produce graduates whose competencies meet world-class standards.

College of Business, Management, & Accountancy

The College of Business, Management, & Accountancy (CBMA) is a dynamic and output-oriented college committed to excellence in teaching, research, and community services. To this end, the college is geared towards toward producing globally competitive graduates who are prepared to adequately meet the ever-growing manpower demands in government, business, and industry—locally and globally.

College of Education

The College of Education (COED) is designed to produce teachers who are professionally competent and dynamic in the field of teaching. Guided by the vision-mission of the university, the College of Education is geared towards the preparation of professionally competitive and dynamic teachers, equipped with academic-research based learning and skills, and transformed with the Lasallian values of faith, service, and communion.

College of Arts, Sciences & Technology

The College of Arts, Sciences, and Technology (CAST) is committed to facilitating the holistic development of individuals who will become resources of change in today’s global society in the field of humanities, sciences and technology. To this end, the college offers programs that are designed to prepare students to adapt to the fast-paced demands of the world and to hone their research skills in the areas of specialization.

Our Facilities

VETERINARY MEDICINE SCHOOL BUILDING

The Veterinary Medicine School Building was built in 2014 to provide for additional classrooms for the Doctor of Veterinary Medicine Program and to offer affordable, convenient, and secure living spaces to students and partners from the university.

VETERINARY MEDICINE HOSPITAL

Constructed in 2009, the 739 sq. m. four-storey new Veterinary Medicine Hospital houses two classrooms and two laboratories for the Doctor of Veterinary Medicine Program.

HOTEL DE LA SALLE

Hotel De La Salle is a three-storey, fully-functional facility that was transformed from a veterinary clinic into a mini hotel in 2012. The

THE FORUM

The Forum is a fully-air-conditioned, glass-paneled multi-purpose hall that is utilized for presentation of academic dissertations and conduct of paper conferences. With an area of 150 sq. m., the venue can accommodate 120 people.

PERFORMING ARTS HALL

Completed in 2015, the Performing Arts Hall is a 150 sq. m. state-of-the-art, multimedia hall.

INSTITUTIONAL ACTIVITY CENTER

The Institutional Activity Center has a total area of 1 089 sq. m. and a capacity for 2 000 people.

UNIVERSITY SWIMMING POOL

The University Swimming Pool, constructed in 1979, was renovated in 2016. It is a five-lane, short course indoor-type pool with a maximum depth of 10 feet.

TRACK AND FIELD OVAL

The Track and Field Oval, with a total area of 10 000 sq. m., includes a 300 m.–track and the smallest dimensions allowed for a soccer field

MINI AMPHITHEATRE

Located beside the Track and Field Oval is the Mini-Amphitheatre, an outdoor venue for musical and theatrical performances.
