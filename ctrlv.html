ADDRESS

Situated along Victoneta Avenue in Potrero, Malabon City, De La Salle Araneta University is the only Lasallian university that can be viewed and is directly accessible from the North Luzon Expressway. With a total land area of 54 000 square meters, the campus is a green campus and is the Lasallian school of choice for agriculture, veterinary medicine, and food technology in the Philippines.
